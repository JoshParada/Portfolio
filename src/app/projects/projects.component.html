Pet Guesser

A console-based Pet Guesser. Similar to the 20 Questions game, the user thinks of a common house pet and the bot asks the user a series of yes or no questions to narrow down the possibilities, ultimately ending up at a guess. If the bot guesses incorrectly, you've successfully beaten the bot.
